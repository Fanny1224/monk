\subsection{Detailed Description}
Constants and unit conversions

The library\textquotesingle{}s default unit system is C\+GS. Sometimes constants in SI units (prefixed by {\ttfamily si\+\_\+}) or in geometrical units (G=c=1; prefixed by {\ttfamily gu\+\_\+}) are needed.

To convert between different units, some frequent conversion factors are defined. E.\+g. by multipying an energy value given in ergs by {\ttfamily erg2kev} factor makes a conversion to kiloelectronvolts.
