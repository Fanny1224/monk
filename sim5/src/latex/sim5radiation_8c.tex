Number of photons that are emitted by a black-\/body surface.

Gives total number of photons $\frac{dN}{dt dA d\Omega}$ of all energies that are emitted form a unit surface of a black body of temperatute {\ttfamily T} into a unit solid angle. Assumes either isotropic or limb-\/darkened emission and a color correction of effective temperature by factor {\ttfamily hardf}.
